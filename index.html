About Me

I am a mom first. Everything else, the career, the degree, the late nights, gets built around that.

I work in ERP consulting, which basically means I help manufacturing companies get their business software working the way their people actually need it to. I am on track to finish a B.S. in Computer Information Systems at Texas A&M University-Central Texas in 2026.

Outside of work I love live music, Broadway, Oscar season, a good shopping trip, and going somewhere new whenever I can.

Based in the Austin area, TX.

Inspiration

My dad walked so I would keep going.

He walked the stage at the University of Houston in 2022, somewhere around 62. He did not make a big thing of it. He just finished. And that was the whole point.

Watching him do that made it harder for me to make excuses about my own degree. So I stopped.
